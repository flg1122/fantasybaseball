🏆 Top Performer of the Week

Moltisantis Mistress – 812.77 pts

Starting off with our top scorer of the week: Moltisantis Mistress.

Moltisantis Mistress dropped 800+ points, the highest score of the week by a mile, and beat the absolute shit out of Slammingmuf.

Safe to say: Moltisantis Mistress has arrived after beginning the season 1-2.

1️⃣ Matchup of the Week

Justin (701) defeats Bryce (680)

Despite MANAGERIAL MALPRACTICE, with Justin leaving Will Warren and his ridiculous 48.5 points on the bench, he still took down the undefeated Bryce and handed him his first loss of the season.

Sound familiar? Last year Justin knocked off Bryce in the semifinals. At this point, Bryce may need a new strategy because Justin is becoming a full-on problem.

😬 Closest Matchup of the Week

VinSalv (526) defeats Sarrel (512)

Heading into Sunday, it looked like Sarrel was finally on his way to his first win of the year. Then VinSalv dropped 119 points in the final hours and flipped the whole thing.

Sarrel only lost by 14 points, and it is safe to say his Yankees did him no favors: Camilo Doval posted -11 and Luis Gil added -8.

It is getting late early for Sarrel, and it may be time to put fandom aside if he wants to turn this around.

🔁 Revenge Game Completed

Team Lill vs Johnny Sack

Last season, Johnny Sack knocked Team Lill out in the semifinals, and that clearly was not forgotten.

This week, Team Lill got his get-back and put an absolute beatdown on Johnny Sack, 725-603.

A solid win, payback, and maybe the start of something bigger if these two cross paths again later this season.
