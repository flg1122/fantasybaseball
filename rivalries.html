Bad Blood Department

Rivalries Are Building

Every league needs tension. Every season needs grudges. Some beef is already established. Some is still simmering. Either way, the matchups are starting to mean more.

🔥 Rivalry Heat Scale
🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥 Nuclear

Borderline personal hatred. Managers checking each other’s scores every 10 minutes.

🔥🔥🔥🔥 Raging

High-end teams, playoff implications, and enough history to make every matchup matter.

🔥🔥🔥 Heating Up

The bad blood is building. One more brutal matchup could push this into full warfare.

🔥🔥 Simmering

Competitive tension exists, but somebody still needs to truly break the other guy’s heart.

🔥 Mild Beef

Friendly shots for now. But every rivalry has to start somewhere.

Main Event JohnnySack leads 3–1

JohnnySack vs Justin

JohnnySack got the best of Justin when it mattered most, beating him in last year’s championship. But Justin got one back this year, taking their only matchup so far.

  • 2025 Week 8: JohnnySack 760, Justin 618
  • 2025 Week 17: JohnnySack 897, Justin 746
  • 2025 Championship: JohnnySack 660, Justin 469
  • 2026: Justin over JohnnySack

JohnnySack is 3–1 lifetime against Justin, but this rivalry is just heating up. Championship history, Week 6 drama, and plenty of crying on both sides.

New Blood Rivalries Pending

Wagner, VinSalv, Sarrel & Moltisanti’s Mistress

Wagner was pratically a non-factor in this league last year. He has not built any meaningful rivalries yet. However, he has been on a heater this year & I can see some things brewing already.

As for VinSalv, Sarrel, and Moltisanti’s Mistress, they are first timers in this league, so they have not built true rivalries yet.

But as they try to make themselves real threats, some bad blood is sure to build. Moltisanti’s Mistress already looks dangerous, Sarrel finally got in the win column, and VinSalv is trying to prove he belongs.

Give it time. Every villain needs an origin story.
