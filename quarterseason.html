Quarter-Season Draft Check-In

We are just over a quarter of the way through the regular season, 27.3% to be exact. Some draft picks are aging beautifully. Others already look painful.

Max Muncy (A's), Garrett Crochet, Eugenio Suarez, Hunter Greene, Jackson Chourio, Juan Soto, Francisco Lindor, and Spencer Strider have already missed time — testing depth much earlier than expected.


🎯 Final Takeaway
