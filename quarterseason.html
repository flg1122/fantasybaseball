Week 6 Feature

Quarter-Season Draft Check-In

We are just over a quarter of the way through the regular season (27.3% to be exact). I decided to do a quick recap on our draft and some players who are sticking out. Some draft picks are aging beautifully. Others already look painful.

📊 Draft Check-In

27% Through the Season: Hits, Misses & Surprises

💎 Best Value

Yordan Alvarez at Pick 45 by Moltisanti's Mistress might be the best value in the draft so far. Yordan is currently the Number 1 overall player in our league.

🚨 Biggest Surprise

Sal Stewart was VinSalv’s final pick and somehow became his best player.

🤔 Under the Microscope

Shohei Ohtani has been great, but Pick 2 overall comes with unfair expectations.

📉 Early Concern

Aaron Judge is reliable, but managers expect league-breaking numbers from Pick 1.

🚑 Injury Impact

Max Muncy (A's), Garret Crochet, Eugenio Suarez, Hunter Greene, Jackson Chourio, Juan Soto, Francisco Lindor, and Spencer Strider have already missed time — testing depth much earlier than expected.


🎯 Final Takeaway

Value was everywhere in this years draft, if you hit on your mid-to-late rounds. Top picks are not guaranteeing wins, and some teams are maximizing their draft far better than others.
